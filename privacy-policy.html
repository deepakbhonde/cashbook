Privacy Policy

Simple Cash Book

Last Updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

Simple Cash Book does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as email address, phone number, or location.

Local Data Storage

All transactions, reminders, settings, and records are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your data on any server.

Backup & Restore

The app allows users to create, download, share, and restore backup files. Backup files are managed entirely by the user.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind users about scheduled reminders. Notification data remains on the device and is not transmitted externally.

Internet Usage

Most features of Simple Cash Book work completely offline. An internet connection is not required for daily use.

Third-Party Services

When users choose to share backup files through services such as Google Drive, Gmail, WhatsApp, or similar applications, those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Your information remains on your device unless you intentionally export or share it.

Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
