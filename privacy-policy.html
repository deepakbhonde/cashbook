# Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 2026 Simple Cash Book ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App. ## Information We Collect The App does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location. All financial records, transactions, reminders, and settings entered into the App are stored locally on your device. ## Data Storage The App stores your data on your device using local storage. Your information is not transmitted to our servers. You are solely responsible for maintaining backups of your data. ## Backup and Restore The App may allow you to export, share, or restore backup files. Backup files are created and managed by you. When you share a backup file using third-party apps such as Google Drive, WhatsApp, Gmail, or other services, their privacy policies apply. ## Notifications The App uses local notifications to remind you about scheduled reminders and due payments. Notification data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to any server. ## Internet Access Most features of the App work completely offline. The App does not require an active internet connection for normal operation. ## Third-Party Services The App may interact with third-party services only when you choose to share backup files or access external links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. ## Data Security Your data remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it. While reasonable measures are taken to ensure proper operation of the App, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. ## Children's Privacy The App is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date. ## Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact: Email: [support@directlychat.com](mailto:support@directlychat.com) Website: cashbook.directlychat.com
